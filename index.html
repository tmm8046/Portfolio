My name is Tommy Murray, Jr. and I major in Game Design and Development in the Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). One of my main goals is to become a lead creative director in the video game or the movie industry to produce content that people will love and enjoy. I also hope to finish writing my science fiction novel. Some of my hobbies include playing video games, watching movies, reading novels, writing, drawing, traveling and running.
